Cosmetic dentistry has helped patients all over the world correct common dental imperfections and completely renew their smiles! Here at Coronado Dental Group, Dr. Jimmy Wu can perform treatments such as porcelain veneers, teeth whitening, dental implant restoration, or other cosmetic and general dental care options to improve both the form and function of your teeth.

Cosmetic Dentist - Dr. Jimmy C. Wu

"Dentistry has always been thought of as something you have to do versus something you want to do. My goal is to change that perception of dentistry and increase the awareness of the benefits of what modern dentistry has to offer."

Our cosmetic dentist, Jimmy Wu, DDS, grew up right here in San Diego, and has been practicing cosmetic dentistry for more than 15 years. After graduating with his dental degree from the University of Southern California, he opened Coronado Dental Group, a comprehensive cosmetic dentistry practice focusing on providing patients with state-of-the-art dental procedures in a soothing and comfortable environment. Dr. Wu is a member of many respected dental organizations, and has completed thousands of hours of continuing dental education in order to offer his patients the latest treatment techniques.
