Our practice is committed to providing affordable dental services which attend to the diverse needs of our valued patients and provide the relief they deserve. From optional cosmetic services to the oral surgeries you need, we stand prepared to care for your needs today.

Please Contact a member of our professional consultative staff directly in order to discuss how we can diagnose and treat your dental needs today!

Cosmetic Dental Care

Our cosmetic dentistry procedures can renew your smile with beautiful results. Invisalign® clear teeth aligners, whitening procedures for even the deepest of stains, and custom-designed porcelain veneers are just some of the popular treatments we offer at Sutra Dental Spa. Our cosmetic dental care options ultimately blend the worlds of science and art to meet your needs and restore confidence in your smile.

Reconstructive Dental Care

If a dental issue is affecting the function of your teeth or the appearance of your smile, one of our advanced restorative dental procedures may be the ideal solution. Dr. Wu offers a number of procedures designed to address missing teeth, decayed or damaged teeth, and other common concerns with results that look and feel natural. Options include dental implants, implant-supported dentures, and more.

Preventive Dental Care

Preventive dental care can keep your teeth and gums healthy and reduce the potential for major dental problems later in life. Our practice offers an array of general dentistry procedures that can help you maintain excellent oral health and a refreshed-looking smile. Learn about professional teeth cleanings, tooth-colored fillings, periodontal care, and many other options.

Advanced Dental Care

We believe in going the extra mile for our patients to ensure each of their needs is met. Our advanced dental care techniques allow us to do just that. Sutra Dental Spa offers oral conscious sedation dentistry to alleviate dental anxiety; treatment for TMJ Disorder; and customized solutions for sleep apnea and snoring. Plus, our practice utilizes laser dentistry and other state-of-the-art technologies to help make dental treatments as precise and comfortable as possible.
