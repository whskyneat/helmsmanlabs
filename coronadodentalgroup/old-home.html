San Diego Dentist:
Dr. Jimmy C. Wu

Dr. Jimmy Wu is a San Diego native and began his dental practice in 2000. In 2005, Dr. Wu partnered with a friend who owned a dental lab, moved his practice to its current location, and brought all of his original patients along with him, establishing Coranado Dental Group. Dr. Wu is passionate about serving his local community and has an extensive background in the areas of cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, and CEREC® ceramic fillings. With his experience as a CEREC® dentist, Dr. Wu has placed over one thousand porcelain veneers and CEREC®restorations, as well as completed hundreds of full mouth reconstruction cases.
